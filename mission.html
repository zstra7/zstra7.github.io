Our Mission

“Smile and Move” personal training offer private personalized sessions with all the focus on you. With gyms becoming over populated, many individuals struggle to take their first steps towards a healthier lifestyle as it can be intimidating and frightening walking into a commercial gym. We provide all sessions privately in our fully equipped shed to ensure a comfortable, private setting. Our number one aim at Smile and Move is to create a positive and happy environment for people of all ages and fitness standards. With a flexible timetable between two trainers, we work together to fit around you and make sessions possible. We are real, so we understand challenges you may face to keep a healthy lifestyle while having a busy schedule. Whatever your goals may be, Smile and Move provide accountability, motivation and expertise to achieve the best possible results.
