Meet The Trainers


Danielle and Sam

We met each other through University, we both share the same passion and have similar traits. We loved spending time together so much we decided to start a business together which we called Smile and Move because we both smile a lot. Going to uni together and working together wasn’t enough we decided to live together and turned our double garage to a privet studio for our members

We feed off each other’s ideas and reflections to build our continuous learning which make us a great team (our lecture at uni even call us the Dream Team!)
