The Good Food List

These foods have been shown to be 'safe'.

Please note

If allergies to fish, nuts, salicylates, etc, please omit them.

A Third of your meals should consist of raw vegetables the more the better.

To avoid parasites l do recommend cooking meat and fish also steaming is better than boiling your vegetables.

It is Important to have a wide variety of foods with as many different fruit and vegetables possible.

Fruit

Fresh

Lemons, apples, mandarins, pears, paw paws, mangoes, grapes, cantaloupes, pineapples, bananas, cherries, blueberries, blackberries, cranberries, mulberries, honey dew, figs, apricots, nectarines, plums, persimmons, guava, kiwifruit, peach, grapefruit, Raspberries strawberries and watermelon.

Vegetables

Fresh

Carrots, cabbage, cauliflower, avocado, pumpkin, broccoli, leeks, radish, parsnip, onion, celery, lettuce, peas, beans, squash, spinach, sprouts, silver beet, mushrooms, cucumber, asparagus, endive, shallots, kale, coconuts, rocket, marrow, Watercress and English Spinach.

Nuts

Macadamia, almonds, peanuts, walnuts, pecan, Hazelnuts, (Preferably unshelled) cashews, Organic Pumpkin seeds, sesame seeds, sunflower seeds.

Herbs

Preferably fresh

Garlic, ginger, chives, parsley, thyme, rosemary, oregano, sweet basil, marjoram, watercress, bush basil, horseradish, mint, salad burnet, dill, chervil, anise, balm, borage, caraway, sage, sorrel, dandelion.

Oils

Use cold pressed oils

Avocado, Macadamia for tasty salad dressing and Olive Oil extra virgin for cooking (or dressing) recommended for healthy diets.

Cod Liver Oil, Organic Flaxseed Oil, Apple Cider Vinegar.

Pasta

Ingredients Rice and water are usually safe

Rice Pasta, Rice Noodles, Rice Paper and Rice (white polish) Quinoa.

Meat

Lean preferably

Beef, Lamb and Veal.

Poultry and Dairy

Free-Range Eggs, organic chickens, dairy products, butter, natural yogurt.

Seafood

Fresh or frozen

Squid, Cuttlefish, Tuna, Trevally, Gemfish, Snapper, John Dory, Bream, Salmon.....(omega 3 oils)

Tinned Fish

Preferably in brine salt and water

Sardines, Salmon, Herrings.

Beverages

Preferably in brine salt and water

Water (most important), Freshly squeezed fruit or vegetable juice, any from the above list. Herbal teas. (Read labels most have additives) Are best made from fresh herbs. Green Tea, dandelion tea.

Other

Peppercorns, Rock or sea Salt, Organic Tahini (pure sesame seeds), Organic Honey.
